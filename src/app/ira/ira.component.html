What is an ira?


An IRA (individual retirement account) is a personal, tax-deferred account the IRS created to give investors an easy way to save for retirement.

 Frequently Asked Questions Q: What is the maximum contribution ? You can contribute a maximum of $6,000 to a Traditional IRA, if under 50 yrs old and a maximum of $7,000 for those 50 yrs old and over. Q: What are the penalties for early withdrawl ? There are no penalties if you withdraw before the age of 72, however withdrawing before 59 1/2 yrs old, there is a 10% penalty and additional income tax on the withdrawl.
